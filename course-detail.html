Course Details

This comprehensive Project Management course will help you develop the skills to successfully plan, execute, and manage projects of any size.

You'll learn the most important concepts including:

  1. Introduction to Project Management fundamentals
  2. Creating and managing essential project documentation (Project Charter)
  3. Effective scope management techniques
  4. Budget planning and control
  5. Project scheduling and timeline management
  6. Risk assessment and mitigation strategies
  7. Team leadership and stakeholder management
  8. Project closing and lessons learned

This course is designed for:

  • New or aspiring Project Managers
  • Team leaders looking to enhance their project coordination skills
  • Professionals who manage projects as part of their role
  • Anyone interested in learning practical project management skills

Course Highlights:

  • Learn to balance the triple constraints: Cost, Time, Quality, and Scope
  • Practical exercises that reinforce key concepts

Prerequisites:

  • Basic computer skills (0.5 years experience)
  • Familiarity with common tools like MS Office or Google Suites
